Our Services

Sharqiah Labs delivers advanced AI solutions for the oil & gas industry.

Drilling & Completions AI

Real-time drilling optimization using machine learning to analyze drilling parameters, predict potential issues, and recommend optimal drilling practices. Our AI systems help reduce non-productive time and improve wellbore quality.

Predictive Analytics

Comprehensive predictive modeling for equipment maintenance, production forecasting, and risk assessment. Our AI solutions help operators anticipate problems before they occur and optimize operations for maximum efficiency.

Digital Transformation

End-to-end digital transformation consulting for oil and gas companies. From data strategy to AI implementation, we help organizations leverage their data assets to gain competitive advantages in an evolving energy landscape.

Expertise

Deep domain knowledge in oil & gas, AI, and data science.

Geoscience AI Solutions

Advanced machine learning algorithms for seismic interpretation, geological modeling, and prospect identification. Our AI systems can process vast amounts of geophysical data to identify promising exploration targets and reduce drilling risks.

From seismic attribute analysis to fault detection and stratigraphic interpretation, our solutions leverage deep learning to extract insights that traditional methods might miss.

Reservoir Engineering Intelligence

AI-powered reservoir simulation and production optimization. Our systems integrate real-time data from multiple sources to provide dynamic reservoir management solutions that maximize recovery while minimizing costs.

Predictive analytics for production forecasting, waterflood optimization, and enhanced oil recovery techniques. We help operators make data-driven decisions that directly impact field performance and economic outcomes.
